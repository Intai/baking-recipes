- Dice quarter onion
- Whip an egg
- Preheat oven to 230°C with oven tray
- Sift 2 cups standard flour, 3 tsp baking powder, 0.5 tsp of salt, 1 tsp of sugar into a bowl
- Mix dry ingredients, diced onion and grated cheese together by a chop stick
- Pour in 1 cup of milk and mix lightly
- Dust flour, shape into a flat rectangle, and then transfer onto baking paper
- Cut into 8 cubes using a sharp knife
- Egg wash on top
- Bake for 15 minutes
